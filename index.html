Short Bio

I am a senior research engineer at Huya Live. I received my bachelor degree from Xidian University, and my Ph.D degree from Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(SIAT),Chinese Academy of Science(CAS), under the supervision of Prof.Baoquan Chen and Prof. Hui Huang. Before joining Huya, I worked at Autonomous Driving Technology Unit(ADT),Baidu. Prior to that, I worked with Prof. Peter Wonka as a postdoc at KAUST.
