Short Bio

Feilong is a senior research engineer at Autonomous Driving Business Unit(ADU),Baidu. Before that he worked with Prof. Peter Wonka as a postdoctoral fellow at KAUST. He received his Ph.D degree from Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(SIAT),Chinese Academy of Science(CAS), under the supervision of Prof.Baoquan Chen and Prof. Hui Huang in 2015, and his bachelor degree from Xidian University in 2008. 


Research Interests

Computer Graphics and Computer vision, especially the acquisition,reconstruction,perception of 3D scenes.
